CryptoTracker Overview

Overview

Our system,Crypto Trackeris an application that monitors cryptocurrencies, displays graphs and news about their development, offers tools that allow users to choose when they want to invest or withdraw their cryptocurrencies and warns any potential user of when it is a good opportunity to invest.

This is achieved by relying in multiple interconnected modules responsible for getting info from external sources (news and data about coins), storing it in our database and then make it available to the users. We also have a machine learning module that, based on the data collected previously, gives tips about the best opportunities to users invest or withdraw the invested money.

Features:

  • Keep yourself informed about the different CryptoCurrencies through the different graphs
  • Create an Alarm notification sent directly to the front-end or by email about a certain crypto
  • Keep yourself informed about your favourite CryptoCoin by having tailored news
  • Up your game of investment with a machine learning prediction algorithm
